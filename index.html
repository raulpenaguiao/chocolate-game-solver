Welcome to the Chocolate Game!

Two players take turns breaking off pieces of a chocolate bar.

The bar is shown as a grid of squares — each column represents a stack of pieces.

How to Make a Move

Click any square (shown in red). That square and all pieces to the right and below it break off (grayed out).

What remains is the next player's bar.

Losing Condition

The player who is forced to take the last piece loses!

Play smart — can you find the winning strategy?

Using the App

  • Use Create Rectangular Partition to start with a new chocolate bar.
  • Click squares in the display panel on the right to make a move.
  • Press Calculate to check if you are in a winning or losing position.
